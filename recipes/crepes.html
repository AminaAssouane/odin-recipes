Crepes

Description

This is a simple vanilla Crepes recipe, you can do it at home for only 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 200g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 1ts sugar
  • 1ts oil
  • a little bit of salt
  • a little bit of vanilla

Steps:

  1. You first crack the eggs and them in a bowl.
  2. Then you pour the milk and the oil.
  3. And then you mix.
  4. you then add the sugar, the oil, the salt and the vanilla, then the flour progressively while stirring.
  5. They have to be cooked on frying pan that you had previously buttered so that the dough doesn't stick.
